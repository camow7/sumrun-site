SPEED!

Test your mathematical reflexes in SumRun Speed, where every second counts! Tackle twelve increasingly complex equations before the 5-minute timer runs out.

Start with four quickfire puzzles, selecting the perfect operators to match the target number in the center. Remember—calculations flow clockwise, so think fast but strategically!

Successfully solve these, and you'll face four new challenges with added complexity. The final four puzzles will push your speed-solving skills to the limit with three critical operator placements.

Can you harness both speed and precision to complete all twelve clockwise equations before time expires? Challenge yourself, challenge your friends—SumRun Speed turns mathematical prowess into an adrenaline-pumping race against the clock!

CHANCE

Luck meets logic in SumRun Chance—where strategy and fortune collide! Face the challenge of solving clockwise equations, but with a thrilling twist of fate.

At each level, choose wisely from four equations. One special "Gate Node" holds the key to advancement—solve it correctly, and you'll instantly leap to the next level without tackling the remaining equations!

With perfect luck and sharp skills, you might complete all three increasingly difficult levels by solving just three equations instead of twelve! But choose carefully—the Gate Node's identity remains hidden until you make your selection.

Master the clockwise order of operations, trust your mathematical instincts, and perhaps Lady Luck will smile upon your calculations. Will you play it safe or gamble on finding the shortcut? In SumRun Chance, sometimes the fastest path to victory isn't solving every equation—it's finding the right one!
